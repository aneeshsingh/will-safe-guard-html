About Us

RJ Thomas is constantly up to date in relation to the government subsidies, first home owner grants and stamp duties exemptions or concessions that are available.

Whether you’re buying or selling or getting a mortgage you can contact us by phone or e-mail and we will respond immediately. We keep our customers informed and up to date at all times.
